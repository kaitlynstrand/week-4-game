  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game

  • There are four gemstones below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score

  • You can win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

  • The value of each gemstone is hidden until you click on it, so keep an eye on them!

  • Each time the game restarts, the value of the gemstones will be reset
